What is tennis?

Tennis is a sport played between two players (singles) or two pairs (doubles) on a rectangular court. Each player uses a racket to hit a felt ball over a net into the opponent's side. The goal is simple — hit the ball so your opponent can't hit it back. The first player to win enough points, games, and sets wins the match. That's the whole sport at its core!
